Detailed Description

Determines closest Triangle, Edge, or Vertex object to a given point.

Computes a distance vector to every Triangle and Vertex object found and stores the object with shortest distance.

The distance to a Vertex object is measured directly from the search point to the Vertex's coordinates. The interpolation weight to a vertex is one.

The distance to a Triangle is measured from the search point, to a point orthogonally projected onto the triangles plane (or line in 2D). The distance is valid only then, when the projected point lies within the triangle, i.e. when the barycentric coordinates of the projected point are all smaller or equal to one. The interpolation weigths for the points of the triangle are equal to the barycentric coordinates.
